üì∏ Open Products Facts, üß¥ Cosmetics, üêæ Pet Food & More

The app is now your portal to the "Wikipedia of all products." We've merged Open Food Facts, Open Beauty Facts, Open Pet Food Facts, and more into one universal platform.

  • Universal Search: Search for food, cosmetics, pet food, and more, all from one app, with access to over 4 million products.
  • Folksonomy Engine: Add extra information to *any* product, like its width, height, color, spicyness level (the infamous Scoville scale), and much more.
  • Create Custom Lists: Organize your products into private lists. You could create a list for your "Morning Routine" or "Living Room Items".
  • Private & Sharable: Your lists remain private on your phone by default, but can be easily shared with anyone you want or exported.
  • Carbon Footprint: New integrations to track and display carbon footprint data for a wider range of products.
